Bulletbox 26.1.1-03a
In Beta Testing!
Stuctures
  • New file Structure.cs. (26.1.1-03a.1/1)
  • New textures: resources/textures/structure/center.png, resources/textures/structure/raidshroom_field.png, resources/textures/structure/soccer_net.png. (26.1.1-03a.1/2)
  • Raid outposts now generate every ~20,000 chunks. (26.1.1-03a.1/3)
  • Raid outposts have a tag to mark whether it's original raid has been completed yet. (26.1.1-03a.1/4)
  • Raid outposts only generate every 120 chunks away at minimum. (26.1.1-03a.2/10)
  • Raid outposts have a 120-chunk circular border around them, to prevent players from running away. (26.1.1-03a.1/5)
Raiders and Raids
  • Raids no longer start randomly (26.1.1-03a.2/1)
  • The raid countdown bar has been removed. (26.1.1-03a.2/2)
  • Raids can now only have 2-3 raiders (rarely 4). (26.1.1-03a.2/3)
  • Raids now start once the player is 60 chunks/960 units away or less from a raid outpost. (26.1.1-03a.2/4)
  • Added a new "Raid Aproaching..." bar with animations. (26.1.1-03a.2/5)
  • Moved all raid context to the Player class. (26.1.1-03a.2/6)
  • Fixed raider naming. (26.1.1-03a.2/7)
  • Fixed Delta Time. (26.1.1-03a.2/8)
  • Fixed the Raid Encounter bossbar. (26.1.1-03a.2/9)
  • Raiders can no longer escape bounding areas. (26.1.1-03a.2/10)
  • Raiders no longer show their nametags. (26.1.1-03a.2/11)
  • Raiders are slightly less directly towards the player and strafe more. (26.1.1-03a.2/12)
Particles & Effects
  • Added a new damage splash particle. (26.1.1-03a.3/1)
  • Added a new DamageParticle to Playing.cs. (26.1.1-03a.3/2)
  • Added a vignette effect for low health, pulsing. (26.1.1-03a.3/3)
  • Added a shake on the health bar at low (<20) health. (26.1.1-03a.3/4)
Hunger and Dash Bars
  • Added 11 new textures: 0.png through 110.png, in resources/textures/ui/hunger_bar/ (26.1.1-03a.4/1)
  • Added a new hunger bar to the right of the health bar. (26.1.1-03a.4/2)
  • Added a automatic regeneration feature, where you lose hunger but gain health. (26.1.1-03a.4/3)
  • You can now right click raidshrooms to consume them, increasing your hunger. (26.1.1-03a.4/4)
  • The server now knows when you eat raidshrooms, fixing an infinite food source. (26.1.1-03a.4/5)
Other / Miscellaneous
  • A new sound has been added at resources/sounds/ui/textures_loaded.mp3. (26.1.1-03a.5/1)
  • Updated textures for beach_umbrella, desert_log, large_tree, meadow_hedge, oasis_desert, palm_tree, sailboat, small_tree, stone, and tumbleweed. (26.1.1-03a.5/2)
  • Added a new World class beside the ServerWorld. (26.1.1-03a.5/3)
  • You can now put any item in your offhand. (26.1.1-03a.5/4)
  • The offhand doesn't glitch behind your player at some angles. (26.1.1-03a.5/5)
  • Lowered the 150 degree rotation of offhand items to 45 degrees (26.1.1-03a.5/6)
  • Removed the "Open to LAN" button as it has many bugs in need of fixing. This will be added back in a future snapshot. (26.1.1-03a.5/7)
  • Moved the health bar slightly up. (26.1.1-03a.5/8)
  • Forced logging in when user_data doesn't exist, or when HasLoggedIn on user_data is false. (26.1.1-03a.5/9)
  • The splash screen now loads user data. (26.1.1-03a.5/10)
  • Added 3 new tutorials to the game, which help assist the player in getting started. (26.1.1-03a.5/11)
  • Entities now have colission. (26.1.1-03a.5/12)
  • Added version and legal info to the main ui screens. (26.1.1-03a.5/13)
