Bulletbox 26.1 Pre-Release 3
Pre-Release
With this pre-release, we are adding a few more advancements, per-world advancements, and better world save systems.
New Advancements
  • Rock Bottom (Obtain a rock)
  • Oxidized (Obtain copper)
  • Iron Age (Obtain iron)
  • Reinforced (Upgrade a tool to iron)
  • First Blood (Kill a mob)
  • Getting Stronger (Kill 25 mobs)
  • Crystal Clear (Obtain quartz)
  • Enough Crystals Already (Obtain 20 quartz)
  • Thats Enough Crystals, No? (Obtain 99 quartz)
  • Stop It With The Crystals (Obtain 198 quartz)
  • I'm Hungry (Obtain 20 raidshrooms)
  • FOOOOOOOOOOD! (Obtain 99 raidshrooms)
  • Sandy Shores (Visit a beach)
  • Where Am I? (Have an X or Y coordinate of above 5000)
  • Bonk Bonk (Obtain a brimstone kanabo)
  • Thanks, But No Thanks (Make a mob take explosion damage from its own bomb)
  • Survivor (Be at 1 health)
  • What Are You Doing? (Be in the end dimension for over 10 minutes)
  • Who Needs Protection? (Defeat the apex without a shield in your inventory)
  • Touch Grass (Visit a Meadow biome)
  • I Regret Nothing (Stand on lava for 2 seconds)
  • Definitely Prepared (Enter The End with no diamond or brimstone weapons)
  • This Seems Safe (Summon the Brimstalker without any iron or diamond weapons)
Other
  • Fixed a bug where worlds would reset when structures were "corrupted."
  • Fixed several advancement-related bugs.
  • Fixes several advancement-card-related bugs.
  • Fixed several inventory-releated bugs.
  • Added a togleable list of availible crafting recepies.
  • Added per-world advancements alongside the per-user ones.
World Saves
  • Removed the one-world setup, and replaced it with a scrollable world list that has named worlds.
  • Added a saves folder
  • Added 3 screens to handle world selection.
  • The version attribute of the world is now checked to be up to date. If not, it shows a popup warning the player that this world is from another version, giving them two options "Back" and "I know what I'm doing!"
