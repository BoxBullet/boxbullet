Bulletbox 26.1 Pre-Release 2
Pre-Release
With this pre-release, we have revamped the apex boss fight. We also added a new Add-Ons screen with our current add-on, the speedrun timer. Future ideas that we think are good but don't want added to the main game will be added here, along with Experimental additions. We have also added user-specific advancements.
User Interface
  • Replaced the Friends screen with a new Add-Ons screen.
  • Added an Advancements Screen directly below Options on the home screen.
  • Added a volume slider for both music and SFX in the options menu.
  • Added a new music toggle in the options menu.
  • Renamed "Disconnect" in the pause menu to "Save & Quit".
The Apex
  • The Apex now has its own texture set for each stage (resources/textures/entity/apex/[stage1.png, stage2.png, stage3.png, stage4.png, stage5.png])
  • The Apex now has a new stage threshhold system:
    • Stage 1: 100-80% health
    • Stage 2: 80-50% health
    • Stage 3: 50-30% health
    • Stage 4: 30-10% health
    • Stage 5: 10-0% health
  • The Apex now has new AI for each stage:
    • Stage 1: Does melee attacks, backs off, comes back for more attacks, repeat.
    • Stage 2: It now backs off for longer and shoots bombs at you during that time.
    • Stage 3: Stops melee attack entirely. It now does a charge attack, backs off and shoots bombs, and repeats. Upon reaching 50% health, the Apex enters a rage state, dramatically changing its appearance.
    • Stage 4: No longer gives you breaks. Shoots bombs at you much more frequently, charges at you with better aim, and teleports away when it takes damage.
    • Stage 5: Teleports even when its not taking damage to stop you from hitting it, shoots 2-4 bombs at a time, charges every 3 seconds. (Same texture as stage 4)
