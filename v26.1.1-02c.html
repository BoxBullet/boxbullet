Bulletbox 26.1.1-02c
In Beta Testing!
Fixes
  • The game will now ask for permission to open to LAN again if the permission was denied. (26.1.1-02c.1/1)
  • Your options now get saved automatically. (26.1.1-02c.1/2)
  • The game now pauses when on pause screens or options screens, unless LAN is opened. (26.1.1-02c.1/3)
  • The server will now pause when on pause screens or options screens, unless LAN is opened. (26.1.1-02c.1/4)
Visual Bugs
  • Your render distance now gets dynamically increased to fill your screen. (26.1.1-02c.2/1)
  • Biome blending has bee reduced to 3x3 intead of 5x5. (26.1.1-02c.2/2)
  • The biome blending reshesh timer has been removed. (26.1.1-02c.2/3)
Boss Bars & Health Bars
  • The raid bossbar now is calculated using the sum of the health of all raiders divided by the total number of raiders that the raid initially consisted off. (26.1.1-02c.3/1)
  • The health bars above players and raiders have been fixed. (26.1.1-02c.3/2)
  • The health bars above players and raiders have been replaced with the same hearts used to indicate your own health above your hotbar, but out of 3 instead of 10. (26.1.1-02c.3/3)
  • The health bar above your hotbar has been enlarged. (26.1.1-02c.3/4)
Raids
  • The raid bossbar is now drawn round and renamed to "RAID ENCOUNTER." (26.1.1-02c.4/1)
  • Raiders now take advantage of 90% of the range of their weapons. (26.1.1-02c.4/2)
  • Raids no longer start every 60 seconds, and now start every 40-60 seconds. (26.1.1-02c.4/3)
  • resources/sounds/raid/raid_horn.mp3 now plays at the start of each raid. (26.1.1-02c.4/4)
  • Spear-holding raiders are now only a 2% chance, and kanabo-holding raiders are 8%. (26.1.1-02c.4/5)
  • Raids now end when the player dies. (26.1.1-02c.4/6)
Miscellaneous
  • Removed Multiplayer. (26.1.1-02c.5/1)
  • Removed "| C# Edition" from the title. (26.1.1-02c.5/2)
  • The mouse has been replaced with a plus-sign crosshair that is green when you are clicking in range and red when out of range or uncharged. (26.1.1-02c.5/3)
  • The crosshair color changes slightly to be more obvious based on what it is hovering over. (26.1.1-02c.5/4)
  • Added a disconnected screen. (26.1.1-02c.5/5)
  • Added a death screen. (26.1.1-02c.5/6)
  • Added a new AudioManager class. (26.1.1-02c.5/7)
Shields
  • The shield texture has been properly rotated. (26.1.1-02c.6/1)
  • Shields now block 90% instead of 80% of all incoming damage when being raised. (26.1.1-02c.6/2)
  • Shields now slow you down by 45% instead of 50% when being raised. (26.1.1-02c.6/3)
  • resources/sounds/item/shield/block_1.mp3 now plays when a shield blocks an attack. (26.1.1-02c.6/4)
