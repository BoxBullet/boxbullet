Bulletbox 26.1.1-03c
In Development
New Biomes
  • Ashen Wastelands: Desolate ash-covered biome with dark visuals and ambient ash fall particles. (26.1.1-03c.1/1)
  • Lava Pools: Dangerous lava regions that deal damage on contact and emit glowing ember particles. (26.1.1-03c.1/2)
  • Both biomes now feature ember particles that spawn from footsteps and ambient world activity. (26.1.1-03c.1/3)
New Adversaries
  • Brimstalker Boss: Large aggressive boss with bomb attacks and charge ability. (26.1.1-03c.2/1)
  • Flicker Mob: Ethereal teleporting entity that charges and spawns rarely at night. (26.1.1-03c.2/2)
  • Both mobs include idle, angry, and afraid states with scaled rendering (Flicker is 50% smaller). (26.1.1-03c.2/3)
AI & Gameplay
  • Multi-phase charge system added with telegraph behavior before attacks. (26.1.1-03c.3/1)
  • Brimstalker now throws homing bombs that explode on impact or timeout. (26.1.1-03c.3/2)
